Nikah Ceremony

Toast

Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh.

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim.

First and foremost, all praise and gratitude is due to Allah (SWT), the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful, for bringing us together on this special day.

Today marks the beginning of a sacred journey not just between two individuals, but between two families, two paths, now walking side by side. It is a moment of love, intention, and faith. A promise made not just in the presence of those we hold dear, but under the watchful, loving gaze of Allah.

To my Apeke Adeh, you are a gift I prayed for, a calm in the storm, and the beginning of a chapter I can’t wait to write with you. May our union be filled with compassion, understanding, laughter, and barakah.

To our family and friends, your love, your guidance, and your prayers have shaped this moment, and we thank you deeply for being here to witness this moment. Your presence makes it even more meaningful. May Allah bless you all for your endless support and for helping make today a beautiful milestone.

Let this Nikkah be a reminder that marriage is not the union of perfect people, but the joining of hearts with sincere intentions, patience, and faith. Together, may we grow in love, in deen, and in devotion.

May Allah (SWT) bless this union with peace, mercy, and boundless happiness.
Ameen.

Akanni-Ola Akeem Olamide (Arikeuyo)
The Groom
